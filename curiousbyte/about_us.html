About Curious Byte

A small project built for curiosity, learning, and discovery

What is Curious Byte?

Curious Byte is a simple knowledge website that I created in India to share interesting stories, science explanations, technology insights, and hidden facts about how the world works.

Why I created it

I wanted to make learning fun and easy. Instead of boring textbook explanations, I turn ideas into short, simple, and interesting articles that anyone can understand.

What you will find here

You’ll find articles about science, space, programming, history, technology, games, and many more topics that spark curiosity in me.

Every article is written with one goal: to make you think “Wow… I didn’t know that!”

About me

My name is Amritansh Srivastava.

I enjoy building creative projects, learning new programming concepts, and turning curiosity into real working ideas.

Future of Curious Byte

This project is still growing. I plan to add more articles, improve the design, and build new features over time.

My vision is to create a small but powerful learning space on the internet where curiosity never stops.
